Feline

Cats can purr both when they inhale and exhale.

Scientific Name: Felis catus
Average Length: 18 inches
Average Lifespan: 15 years
Habitat: For cats, their natural habitat includes a range of environments,
from deserts to forests, and from grasslands to wetlands.

Commonly referred to as the domestic cat or house cat, is a small carnivorous mammal.
Cats have highly developed senses, including excellent hearing and sense of smell.
They can see better than humans in dark and dim light.
Cats are expert hunters. In the wild they'll spend 6-8 hours a day hunting.
Domesticated cats have been tamed for nearly 10,000 years.
